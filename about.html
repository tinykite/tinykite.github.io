Hello, I'm Dakota

I’m a designer who codes, driven by a deep love of type, technology, and storytelling.

I spent the first several years of my career as a writer and editor for magazines that include American Craft, Yoga International, Monkeybicycle, and Paper Darts. I believe my publishing background helped me develop a compassionate, human-oriented mindset — and I love finding ways in which that experience can drive the creation of inclusive digital experiences.

I'm also a passionate type nerd, and proud to call myself a third-generation letterpress printer. In the past, I've used letterpress as a medium for everything from telling jokes about commonly ignored plants to professing my love of Beyonce lyrics.

You can find me on Instagram, CodePen, and GitHub.
